About Us

Elyndra was established with a vision to redefine luxury hospitality in Sri Lanka while uplifting the communities we serve. As one of the nation’s most prestigious hotel collections, Elyndra offers exceptional career opportunities and strives to foster inclusivity and prosperity for all Sri Lankans, irrespective of background or belief. Our renowned properties set the benchmark for elegance and service, welcoming guests to experience the pinnacle of comfort and sophistication amid Sri Lanka’s breathtaking landscapes. Elyndra’s operations span iconic destinations, contributing to the growth of tourism, local enterprise, and cultural harmony across the island. Guided by a deep commitment to social responsibility, Elyndra partners with national and local organizations to advance economic development and ensure a brighter, more promising future for the people of Sri Lanka. At Elyndra, we believe true luxury is about elevating everyone—guests, team members, and our communities alike.
